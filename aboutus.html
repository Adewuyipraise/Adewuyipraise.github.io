Our Journey So Far


Children Harvest International is a non-profit organisation with the mandate to reach Boys and Girls with the Gospel of the KINGDOM OF GOD!. We ignited our missions
on the 18th Day of the month of October in the year 2000.
We're Located in the following countries in Africa.

1. Nigeria   2. Malawi   3. Ghana   4. Liberia   5. Sierra-Leone
